About Alaska Digital Solutions

Our Story

Alaska Digital Solutions has emerged as a dynamic organization with the aspiration to provide comprehensive, end-to-end solutions across multiple domains including Call Center operations, System Integration, Office Automation, and Business Process Outsourcing to meet your complex business requirements.

Backed by experienced management, proven marketing expertise, and a dedicated team of qualified professionals, Alaska has evolved from being a key player in Web and Multimedia solutions to becoming a "Total Solution Provider" offering integrated turnkey solutions and cutting-edge IT-enabled services.
